About US

Auronius is a tech company and disruptor in the collectibles market aiming to provide a platform that delivers value to both creators and collectors. Starting our journey with a focus on the comic book industry, we have fostered partnerships with esteemed artists, some of whom have worked with industry giants like Marvel and DC. We sell these artists' exceptional works and collaborate with them and other talented creators to generate exclusive content for our platform.

We are not just a company; we are a platform for creators to shine and for fans to get closer than ever to their favorite artists and heroes they create. We're starting with the comic books, with plans to expand to a wider array of collectibles. Our vision? To transform the way collectibles are bought, sold, and cherished.
